Pancake

Description

Pancakes are soft, fluffy flat cakes made from a simple batter of flour, milk, and eggs, cooked on a hot pan until golden.
Theyâ€™re a popular comfort food enjoyed with sweet or savoury toppings for breakfast or dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter or oil
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Add flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt to a bowl
  2. Crack the egg into the bowl
  3. Pour in milk and melted butter/oil
  4. Whisk until smooth (no big lumps)
  5. Heat a non-stick pan on medium heat
  6. Lightly grease the pan
  7. Pour one ladle of batter into the pan
  8. Cook until bubbles form on top
  9. Flip and cook the other side until golden
  10. Remove and serve hot
