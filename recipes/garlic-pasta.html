Creamy Garlic Pasta

Description

Pasta is a versatile dish made from wheat dough, cooked until tender and paired with a variety of sauces and ingredients.
It’s enjoyed worldwide for its comforting taste, endless variations, and quick preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1Pasta (any type)
  • Garlic (3–4 cloves, chopped)
  • Butter
  • Cream or milk
  • Salt & pepper
  • Cheese (parmesan or any you like)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until soft, then drain
  2. Heat butter in a pan on low heat
  3. Add garlic and cook until fragrant (don’t burn it)
  4. Pour in cream or milk and stir gently
  5. Add salt and pepper
  6. Mix in the cooked pasta
  7. Sprinkle cheese on top and serve hot
