Honey Garlic Chicken

Description

Itâ€™s enjoyed worldwide for its comforting taste, endless variations, and quick preparation.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thighs
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
  2. Cook chicken until golden on both sides
  3. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds
  4. Mix honey and soy sauce, then pour into the pan
  5. Let it simmer until the sauce thickens
  6. Flip chicken to coat well
  7. Turn off heat and serve with rice
